SolveChain - my experiment for judging programming exercises

How will it work? Basics

The SolveChain will be smart contract on Solana blockchain

You will be rewarded of solving programming exercises in native SLV token

On input of the SolveChain you have to provide SOL worth 2$ or SLV worth 1.5$, info on what exercise are you doing and .cpp or .py file

It should be provided by normal submission window on platform supporting SolveChain

On output you will get judged between 0 and 100 points

If you will get ≥85 points, you will get (points+20)% of 2$ in SLV

If you will get ≤84 points, you will get points% of 1.5$ in SLV
